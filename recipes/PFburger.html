Pulp Fiction Burger

Description

This is a succulent juicy burger from Pulp Fiction


The Ingredients needed are:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  • Mayonnaise
  • 4 seeded hamburger buns
  • 4 slices American Cheese
  • 1 beefsteak tomato, sliced into 1/4-inch-thick rounds
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Shredded iceberg lettuce
  • ketchup or other condiments

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Preheat grill for direct-heat cooking. Gently form beef into 4 patties, each 4 1/2 inches in diameter and 1/2 inch thick (do not overwork meat or patties will be tough). Season generously with salt and pepper. Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise onto the cut sides of each bun.
  2. Step 2: Place patties on grill and cook, uncovered, until browned and sizzling on the bottom, 3 to 4 minutes. Flip, top each with a slice of cheese, cover grill, and cook until cheese is melted and patties are medium-rare, 2 to 3 minutes more. Transfer to a plate. Place buns on grill cut-side down; cook just until lightly toasted and golden brown in places, about 1 minute.
  3. Step 3: Dress the cut side of each bottom bun with ketchup and mayonnaise (or desired condiments), then top with patties. Place a tomato slice on top of each patty and season with salt. Stack with pickles and lettuce. Dress cut side of top buns with condiments, then sandwich toppings and serve.
